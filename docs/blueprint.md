# **App Name**: Al-Hameed Jewelers

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Display gold jewelry with detailed descriptions, images, and pricing.
- AI-Powered Product Recommendations: Provide personalized product recommendations to users based on their browsing history and purchase patterns. This feature will incorporate a tool that enables it to take factors like user browsing behavior, and the current price of gold into consideration to provide highly relevant results.
- Secure User Authentication: Enable users to create accounts, log in securely, and manage their profiles.
- Shopping Cart and Checkout: Allow users to add products to a cart, review their order, and complete the checkout process securely.
- Payment on Delivery: Payment will be POD ( Payment on Delivery )
- Order Management: Enable users to track their order history, view order details, and manage their shipping addresses.
- Admin Dashboard: Provide Hassan with a dashboard to manage products, orders, and customer accounts.
- Fully Responsive Web-App: Fully Responsive Web-App
- Dynamic and Animated: Fully dynamic, animated, smooth

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep gold (#D4AF37) for luxury and sophistication.
- Background color: Near-black (#121212) to provide contrast and elegance.
- Accent color: Soft gold (#E5C477) for highlighting important elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif typeface for a luxurious and elegant look.
- Use refined, minimalist icons in gold to represent different product categories and actions.
- Use a clean and modern layout with ample spacing to create a sense of luxury.
- Subtle animations and transitions to enhance user experience and add a touch of elegance.
